First Day of School

I remember holding my dad's hand tightly, scared yet excited. The smell of new books, crayons, and my lunchbox is still fresh in my mind.

Hide and Seek

Every evening, the neighborhood kids gathered to play. The thrill of hiding and being found was unbeatable!

Birthday Surprises

My mom baked a chocolate cake and decorated the house with balloons. I felt like a star every year.

Cartoon Sundays

Waking up early with a bowl of cereal and watching Tom & Jerry—pure happiness!

Rainy Day Games

Staying indoors, making paper boats, and jumping in puddles once the rain slowed—it was all joy!

Learning to Ride a Bike

Fell a dozen times but never gave up. The moment I balanced on two wheels was magic!

Grandma's Tales

Each night, she’d tell stories of kings and animals that sparked my imagination endlessly.

School Picnic

Bus rides with friends, singing songs, sharing snacks—it was the best school day of the year.

Festival Fun

Wearing new clothes, lighting candles, and enjoying sweets during every festival with family.

Tree Climbing

We'd climb mango trees, get scratched, and still feel on top of the world. It was our jungle gym.
